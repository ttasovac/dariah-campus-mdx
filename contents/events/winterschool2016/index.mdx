# Winter School

## An open access synthesis of the DARIAH Winter School, 24th-28th October 2016, Prague, Czech Republic

The main topic is the evolution of publication issues in social sciences and
humanities, in a context of Open Access, with the underlying goal of promoting
open science through the question of _open data citation_.
